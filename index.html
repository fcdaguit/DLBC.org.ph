    About DLBC

    Our Mission

    To love God, love one another, and grow disciples rooted in His Word — right here in Barangay Libas and beyond.

    Devine Love Bible Church began as a small gathering of families in Barangay Libas, Merida, Leyte, meeting to worship and study Scripture together. Today we remain a close community church, committed to Sunday worship, daily devotion, and serving our neighbors along the coast we call home. Whether you're a longtime member or visiting Merida for the first time, there's a seat for you.

    Pastor [Rogelio Jordan]
    Senior Pastor, DLBC
